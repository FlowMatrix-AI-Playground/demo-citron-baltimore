Our Story

A decade on the water.

Seasonal, Scratch-Made

Executive Chef Mary Hersh leads a kitchen built on day-boat seafood, center-cut steaks, house-made pastas, and a full sushi bar that changes with the season.

Private Events, Seven Days

Heated waterfront terraces and nine distinct spaces host private events every day of the week, from intimate dinners to a full 500-guest takeover.

An Award-Winning Cellar

Recognized by Wine Spectator every year since 2019, our list pairs deep vintage selections with a menu built to match them, glass by glass.

Forty Years of Catering

Charles Levine has produced nearly 14,000 events for clients including five U.S. presidents, the NFL, the Rolling Stones, and Oprah, now under one roof at Citron.
