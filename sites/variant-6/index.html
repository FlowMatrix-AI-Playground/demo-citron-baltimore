Our Story

Family owned, ten years on the water

"We've catered for five presidents, the NFL, the Rolling Stones, and Oprah, and we still taste every dish before it leaves the kitchen."

Charles and Susan Levine opened Citron Baltimore at Quarry Lake in November 2016, and this November marks ten years on the waterfront. Charles brings more than forty years in the events industry and roughly fourteen thousand catered events to the table, built through Citron Caterers, the family's off-premise catering operation, now in its fourth decade of serving Maryland, DC, and Northern Virginia.

In the kitchen, Executive Chef Mary Hersh leads a scratch-built, seasonal program: center-cut steaks, day-boat seafood, house-made pastas, and a full sushi bar, backed by a wine list Wine Spectator has honored with the Award of Excellence every year since 2019, plus FINE WINE's World's Best recognition in 2025.

Nine Rooms, One Address

Explore every event space

Click a card to see who each room is built for. Capacities are for seated or reception-style events depending on layout.

Fireplace Room

Up to 20

A warm, wood-paneled private dining room built around a working fireplace.

Ideal for: intimate dinners, small board meetings, anniversary parties

Our smallest and most intimate room, the Fireplace Room seats up to twenty around a live-fire hearth. It's the first room we recommend for a milestone birthday dinner or a small family celebration that wants a bit of ceremony without a lot of scale.

Mirror Room

Up to 26

A bright, mirrored private room suited to seated dinners with a formal edge.

Ideal for: rehearsal dinners, engagement parties, executive lunches

Floor-to-ceiling mirrored walls make this twenty-six-seat room feel larger and brighter than its footprint, which is exactly why it's a favorite for rehearsal dinners and engagement celebrations that want a formal, photograph-ready setting.

Club Room

Up to 50

A flexible mid-size room for corporate dinners and milestone celebrations.

Ideal for: corporate dinners, bar and bat mitzvahs, milestone birthdays

The Club Room seats up to fifty and reconfigures easily between boardroom-style corporate dinners and a dance-floor-ready lifecycle celebration. Savant-powered AV is built into the room for presentations or a DJ setup.

Waterfront Terrace

Up to 50

A heated, open-air terrace with direct views over Quarry Lake.

Ideal for: cocktail-hour ceremonies, sunset dinners, engagement parties

Heated for three-season comfort, the Waterfront Terrace puts fifty guests directly on the water for a ceremony, a cocktail hour, or a seated dinner under the open sky, with the main dining room as an easy indoor backup.

Citron Bar

Up to 125

A lively buyout of our bar and lounge for cocktail-style receptions.

Ideal for: happy-hour buyouts, holiday parties, product launches

A full buyout of our handcrafted-cocktail bar and lounge, built for standing receptions up to one hundred twenty-five. It's our go-to recommendation for a corporate holiday party or a launch event that wants energy over formality.

Waterfront Courtyard

Up to 200

An open-air courtyard on the water, ideal for ceremonies and receptions.

Ideal for: wedding ceremonies, large cocktail receptions, outdoor galas

Our largest outdoor footprint, the Waterfront Courtyard holds up to two hundred guests for a ceremony or standing reception with Quarry Lake as the backdrop, and connects directly into The COVE for a seamless move indoors.

The COVE

Up to 200

Our dedicated event ballroom, built for seated dinners and dancing.

Ideal for: weddings, galas, large corporate dinners

The COVE is our purpose-built event ballroom, seating up to two hundred with a dedicated dance floor, staging, and Savant-powered AV and lighting. It's the room behind most of the weddings and galas we host each year.

Entire Venue

Up to 500

A full buyout of Citron Baltimore, indoors and out, for your largest event.

Ideal for: galas, festivals, multi-room corporate summits

Combine the dining room, The COVE, Citron Bar, and both waterfront outdoor spaces for a single event of up to five hundred guests. This configuration is reserved well in advance for our largest fundraising galas and corporate summits.

Off-Premise Catering

Unlimited

Citron Caterers brings our kitchen to your venue, anywhere in the region.

Ideal for: home events, corporate offices, outdoor tented weddings, any venue you already have

With more than thirty years of off-premise experience across Maryland, DC, and Northern Virginia, Citron Caterers can produce full-service catering at a venue of your choosing, at any scale, drawing on the same kitchen and team behind Quarry Lake.
