Our Story

Four decades of hospitality, ten years at Quarry Lake

Charles and Susan Levine opened Citron in November 2016, built on the foundation of Citron Caterers, 30+ years of off-premise catering across Maryland, DC, and Northern Virginia. Charles has personally run more than 14,000 events across his 40-year career, for clients including five U.S. presidents, the NFL, the Rolling Stones, and Oprah.

Executive Chef Mary Hersh now leads a seasonal, scratch kitchen on the water, and Citron marks its 10th anniversary in November 2026, still family owned, still growing.
