Our Story

A family business, ten years on the water

Charles and Susan Levine opened Citron on Quarry Lake in November 2016, built on the catering legacy of Citron Caterers, 30+ years of off-premise events across Maryland, DC, and Northern Virginia. Charles has personally run more than 14,000 events over his 40-year career, for clients ranging from five U.S. presidents to the NFL, the Rolling Stones, and Oprah.

Today, Executive Chef Mary Hersh leads a seasonal, scratch kitchen, and the restaurant celebrates its 10th anniversary in November 2026, still family owned, still on the water.
